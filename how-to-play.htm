How to Play Rock, Paper, Scissors

Rock, Paper, Scissors is a simple hand game played between two people. The rules are:

  • Rock beats Scissors.
  • Scissors beats Paper.
  • Paper beats Rock.

The game is a tie if both players choose the same move.

To play, click the "Rock", "Paper", or "Scissors" buttons and the game will compare your choice with the computer's choice.
